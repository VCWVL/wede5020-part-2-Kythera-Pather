About Us

Who We Are

History Behind: Exclusive books were founded in 1951 in Johannesburg. It is the leading South African bookselling chain known for offering a wide section on book.

We are a community of book lovers dedicated to providing a diverse range of literature, from local authors to international bestsellers. Our goal is to create a welcoming space for readers of all ages and backgrounds.

We believe in the power of books to inspire, educate, and entertain. Our team is passionate about connecting readers with the stories that resonate with them, fostering a love for reading in our community.



Our Values

We value integrity, inclusivity, and a passion for literature. Our commitment to customer service ensures that every visitor feels valued and appreciated. We strive to create an environment where everyone can discover the joy of reading.



Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to inspire a love of reading by providing access to local and international literature. We aim to be a trusted community destination where imagination, knowledge, and creativity are celebrated.

Our vision is a friendly environment where all book lovers and readers can explore and interact.
